Mission

Empowering children by protecting, mentoring, and nurturing them to grow safely, learn fully, and reach their full potential.

Vision

A community where every child is safe, valued, and empowered to thrive and succeed.

Programs

Okoa Watoto serves community children through the following 8 programs:

1. Talents Hub Program

Identify, nurture and showcase children's talents — arts, sports and academics.

2. Mentorship Program

One-on-one mentoring to build skills, confidence and guidance.

3. Positive Parenting

Workshops and support for caregivers to foster healthy family environments.

4. Child Tracing Program

Missing Child / Lost & Found — rapid community tracing and reunification support.

5. Awareness & Advocacy

Community campaigns on child rights, protection and wellbeing.

6. Back To School Program

Supplies, fees and enrollment support so children can learn fully.

7. Health & Nutrition

Health checks, nutrition education and basic support for vulnerable children.

8. Child Protection Referrals

Linking families and children to social services, legal support and shelters.
